Plant Health API

To help users identify plants and detect plant health in their gardens.

Summary

Purpose

The purpose of this case study is to assist individual home growers on identifying plants and the specifics of the plant health through the use of an Application Programming Interface (API). An API is a set of rules and protocols that enable different software applications to communicate and interact with each other. It allows software applications to share data and functionalities with each other in a standardized and organized way.

Problem

As a home-grower, I often face challenges in identifying specific health issues in my crops and indoor plants, especially since there's a risk of these issues spreading throughout the plants.

Project Overview
Background Context

As someone who maintains all the indoor plants at home, I frequently encounter specific plant conditions that require additional information to understand my plants' needs. This exploration could also support my other case study, “One System, One Garden,” particularly during the hydroponics stage for Alpine Strawberries and Bloomsdale Spinach, and could serve as a helpful resource for others building this system. With early detection, we can tackle potential issues immediately, reducing the risk of spread and ultimately preserving plant growth.

Project Timeline

This case study was divided into three project phases for API integration.

| Phase 1:

Research

Research available plant identification APIs and assess features suitable for plant health analysis. Identify necessary data points for plant health assessment.

| Phase 2:

Plant ID API Integration

Set up the API endpoints and integration. Configure the API to process image data to identify and analyze plant health.

| Phase 3:

Testing & Debugging

Conduct testing with various plant images to assess identification accuracy. Set time to debug to achieve desirable results.

Recommendations

When working with APIs, I consider several factors when developing a recommendation. First, I research various machine learning API services to determine which best matches the project's purpose and needs. The API should be straightforward to use and provide clear, concise information relevant to the topic. Additionally, familiarity with JavaScript is important for retrieving data effectively. Finally, it’s essential to allow room for debugging and error handling when integrating the API.

Project Analysis
Contributing Factors to the Issue

As a beginner home-grower, I don’t have much knowledge about the specifics of plant health or the proper steps to address issues that may arise. It can be quite challenging to understand and manage these problems based solely on observation, especially without the help of an API.

Strengths
  • High accuracy with machine learning to identify plants
  • Time efficiency in identifying a plant and plant health right away
  • User accessibility from devices for almost everyone
Weaknesses
  • Quality or the angles of pictures may affect the accuracy of the results or false identification
  • Some API’s databases may be limited to plant species
  • There may be variations of a plant that is significant to a specific location or region and may involve additional research to accurately identify
Steps Taken
  1. Quality or the angles of pictures may affect the accuracy of the results or false identification
  2. Some API’s databases may be limited to plant species
  3. There may be variations of a plant that is significant to a specific location or region and may involve additional research to accurately identify
Concepts

This course has shown me how technology integrates into the agricultural community and highlights its importance. Before, I didn't realize how tech plays a role in this industry, but through this project, I learned that using APIS can support everyone from home-growers to seasoned farmers. With a shared goal of identifying plants and assessing plant health, technology can provide valuable insights and assistance to all.

Challenges

During the integration of the Plant Identifier API onto my webpage, I’ve experienced a couple of debugging and troubleshooting that led to some additional time spent on this case study to produce the desired results.

Solutions

I resolved this challenge by a thorough re-evaluation of my code, working with the HTML and Javascript, and also collaborated with my peers who were facing similar issues and finally came to a conclusion, with a successful working plant identifier API page!
