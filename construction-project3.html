Summary

Purpose

The purpose of this case study is to assist home growers in identifying plants and assessing plant health through the use of an Application Programming Interface (API). An API is a set of rules and protocols that enables different software applications to communicate and interact, allowing them to share data and functionalities in a standardized, organized way.

Problem

As a home-grower, I often face challenges in identifying specific health issues in my crops and indoor plants, especially since there's a risk of these issues spreading throughout the plants.
