03 | CONSTRUCTION DAY

Construction Day

Obtained construction skills at the El Coqui Community Garden.

Summary

Purpose

The purpose of this report is to recognize the construction industry as a practice and obtain construction skills that are valuable to apply to real world situations. The buildout of a small scale project has provided a guideline of the work ethic and culture of this industry. This hands-on approach enhances learning, improves efficiency, and contributes to a successful buildout of projects.

Problem

We had three construction days at the El Coqui Community Garden during the course of the program. During these three days we learned about various construction skills, tools and safety protocols from getting first hand experience on site. Additionally, on each of the three days we used different tools on different materials for a final build that will then be used by the community at the garden.
